Matthew Adrian

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Dr. Jeffrey Gray. My research focuses on diffusion models for biomolecular structure prediction and design.

Previously, I worked at Merck Research Laboratories advised by Dr. Alan Cheng. Here, I developed methods for small-molecule drug property prediction, mainly leveraging graph neural network architectures. I received my B.S. in Chemical Engineering with a concentration in Biotechnology from the University of California, Berkeley.
